Welcome To The Vavuniya Campus

We take great pleasure in welcoming you to the Vavuniya Campus of the University of Jaffna!

Situated in the land that was once part of the kingdom of the legendary Vanni Kings, the Vavuniya Campus is a higher seat of learning and research in this region, which provides higher education in the fields of Applied Science ,Business Management and Technological Studies.

The vision of the Vavuniya Campus of the University of Jaffna is:
To be the centre of excellence in knowledge creation and dissemination through active research. Our mission as a corporate-entity is to see that, we endlessly strive to be the leader in the provision of academic services of excellence in producing readily marketable graduates with intellectual and professional skills who can face the challenges imposed by the 21st century, and, contribute to the sustainable development of the regional, national and international areas.
